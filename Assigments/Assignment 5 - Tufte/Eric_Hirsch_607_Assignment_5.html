Perhaps its something more sinister. Is it possible there is some kind of favoritism going on among airport staff and crew? There is no easy way to measure that directly, but as an albeit weak proxy we observe that Phoenix appears to be some kind of hub for Amwest and Seattle a hub for Alaska:

While Amwest has a higher percentage of delays across the board, is the gap between Amwests and Alaskas delay percentages lowest in Phoenix and highest in Seattle? This might suggest that where Amwest is most prominent they get favored treatment by the airport, and where Alaska is most prominent Amwest gets the least favored treatment.
